Teaching

Microeconomics

University of Oxford

OVERVIEW

This tutorial series ran in the 2021—2022 academic year, serving as a supplementary to the microeconomics lectures for penultimate and final year underguaduate students. This course aims to improve skills of solving technical problems and writting academic essays. It covers general equilibrium, welfare economics, public good and externality, game theory, industrial organisation, decision under risk, and information economics.

OVERVIEW

Introductory Econometrics

Sun Yat-sen University

OVERVIEW

This 12-section course ran in the 2016—2017 academic year, serving as a supplementary to the undergraduate course Econometrics at International Business School (currently, International School of Business and Finance), Sun Yat-sen University. This course aims to enhance the overall understanding of basic econometric terminology, regression methods, and statistical inference. Time series and panel data settings are also discussed.

Summer School

Chang'an University

OVERVIEW

The summer schools were taught in 2022 and 2023 at Chang'an University. Courses include Principle of Microeocnomics, Principle of Macroeconomics, Intermediate Microeocnomics, Intermediate Macroeconomics, Money and Banking, Labour Economics, and Public Finance, aiming to introduce basic terms and concepts to undergraduate students and enhance their ability to analyse daily-life examples using economic theories.
